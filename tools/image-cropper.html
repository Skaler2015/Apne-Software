ℹ️ About This Tool

Crop any image to exactly the area you need, freely or to a fixed aspect ratio like square, 4:3, or 16:9 — useful for profile pictures, thumbnails, or trimming unwanted edges.

How to use:

  1. Upload an image.
  2. Drag the box to position it, and drag the corner handle to resize it. Pick an aspect ratio if you want a fixed shape.
  3. Click Crop & Download to save the cropped result.

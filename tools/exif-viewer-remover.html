ℹ️ About This Tool

Photos taken on phones and cameras often embed hidden EXIF metadata — including the exact GPS location, camera model, and timestamp. This tool shows you what's hidden in a photo and lets you strip it all out before posting or sharing.

How to use:

  1. Upload a photo.
  2. Review the metadata table to see what information is embedded.
  3. Click Remove All Metadata to download a clean copy with no hidden data.

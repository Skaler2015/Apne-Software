ℹ️ About This Tool

Turn any rectangular image into a softly rounded card-style image, or crop it into a perfect circle for avatars and profile pictures. The output is a transparent PNG so it blends into any background.

How to use:

  1. Upload an image.
  2. Choose Rounded Corners or Circle, and adjust the corner radius if needed.
  3. Click Download to save the transparent PNG.

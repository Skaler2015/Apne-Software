🔠 Case Converter

Convert text to UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case or Sentence case.

ℹ️ About This Tool

Convert your text between UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case and other letter cases instantly.

How to use:

  1. Paste or type your text.
  2. Click the case option you want (UPPERCASE, lowercase, etc).
  3. Copy the converted text using the Copy button.

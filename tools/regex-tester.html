ℹ️ About This Tool

Write and test regular expressions against real text, with instant visual feedback. Matches are highlighted directly in your text and listed below with their position and any captured groups.

How to use:

  1. Type your regular expression pattern.
  2. Choose flags — global, case-insensitive, multiline, or dotall.
  3. Paste your test text and see matches highlighted live.

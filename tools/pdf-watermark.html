ℹ️ About This Tool

A watermark stamped across every page of a PDF serves a real purpose: it marks a document as a draft so nobody mistakes it for the final version, it identifies a file as confidential or internal-only before it gets shared further than intended, or it adds a company name or logo across pages to discourage unauthorized copying or to reinforce branding on something like a proposal or report. Adding this by hand to every single page of a long document would be tedious and error-prone, which is exactly the repetitive task this tool automates in a few clicks.

You can add either a text watermark, such as "CONFIDENTIAL", "DRAFT", or a company name, or an image watermark like a logo, and control its opacity, rotation angle, size, and position on the page — diagonal across the center is the classic look for a draft stamp, but you can just as easily place a smaller, more subtle mark in a corner if you want something less intrusive. A live preview shows exactly how the watermark will look on the page before you apply it to the entire document, so there's no surprise after processing every page.

This is genuinely useful across a wide range of situations: legal and financial firms marking drafts before a document is finalized, photographers and designers protecting sample work shared with clients before payment, businesses stamping internal reports as confidential before circulating them more broadly, and students or researchers marking up working drafts of papers to distinguish them clearly from a final submission. Once applied, the watermark appears on every page consistently, so there's no risk of missing a page in a long document.

The entire process runs locally in your browser; your document is never uploaded to a server to have a watermark applied to it, which matters if the underlying content is sensitive even before the watermark is added. Once you're happy with how it looks in the preview, one click generates the watermarked PDF, ready to download immediately, with the original document otherwise completely unchanged apart from the mark you've chosen to add.

How to use:

  1. Upload your PDF.
  2. Type your watermark text and adjust size, color, opacity, rotation.
  3. Apply and download the watermarked PDF.
